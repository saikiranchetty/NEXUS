ACMA’s active involvement in trade promotion, technology upgradation, quality enhancement, and the collection and dissemination of information has positioned it as a vital catalyst for the auto component industry’s growth. ACMA successfully hosted its 64th Annual Session recently, further solidifyingits role in driving industry development. Through participation in international trade fairs, organizing trade delegations, and publishing valuable industry insights, ACMA continues to support the sector’s progress, fostering innovation and global collaboration for a sustainable future.

Mr. Vinnie Mehta,
Director General, ACMA

ACMA stands at the forefront of India’s automotive transformation, embodying a vision that champions innovation, sustainability, and inclusivity. ACMA is not only enhancing the competitiveness of the Indian auto components industry but also positioning it as a vital player in the global mobility landscape. As the automotive ecosystem evolves, ACMA's commitment to collaboration and growth will be crucial in shaping a sustainable and prosperous future for all industry stakeholders.
