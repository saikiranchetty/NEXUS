Our Story

Our History

Founded in 2010 as a family-owned business, PestGuard started with a simple goal: to provide safe and effective pest control services. Over the past 15 years, we’ve grown into a regional leader, serving thousands of homes and businesses across the region. Our commitment to eco-friendly practices and customer satisfaction has earned us a reputation for excellence.

From humble beginnings, we’ve expanded our team, adopted cutting-edge technologies, and achieved numerous industry accolades, all while staying true to our core values of integrity, safety, and quality.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To deliver top-quality pest control services that protect your health, property, and peace of mind using safe, eco-friendly solutions.

Vision: To be the most trusted pest control provider in the region, known for innovation, reliability, and exceptional customer care.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Transparent and honest service in every interaction.
  • Safety: Prioritizing the health of our clients, pets, and the environment.
  • Excellence: Delivering superior results through expertise and innovation.
  • Community: Giving back through education and support initiatives.
