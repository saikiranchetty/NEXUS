Pest Identification Guide

Termites

Small, wood-eating insects that cause structural damage.

Mosquitoes

Blood-sucking pests that thrive in standing water.

Rodents

Mice and rats that invade homes and spread disease.

Bed Bugs

Tiny pests that hide in bedding and cause itchy bites.
