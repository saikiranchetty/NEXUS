Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

Our administrative office is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM, and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. The office remains closed on Sundays and public holidays.

You can schedule a campus tour by calling our admission office at +91 98765 43210 or by filling out the contact form on this page. Tours are typically conducted on weekdays between 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM.

Required documents typically include the student's birth certificate, previous school records (if applicable), passport-sized photographs, address proof, and any relevant medical records. Please visit our Admissions page for a complete checklist.

Fees can be paid through multiple methods: online payment via our school portal, bank transfer, or in-person at the school's accounts office. We accept cash, cheques, and all major credit/debit cards.
