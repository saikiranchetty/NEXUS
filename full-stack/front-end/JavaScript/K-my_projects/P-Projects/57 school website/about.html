About Our Institution

Discover our rich heritage, visionary leadership, and commitment to excellence in education since 1985

Our Story

Founded in 1985 by visionary educator Dr. Rajesh Sharma, Modern Indian School began as a small institution with just 120 students. Today, we proudly serve over 2,500 students across our 8-acre campus, maintaining our founding principles while embracing innovation in education.

Mission

To nurture intellectually curious, socially responsible, and emotionally resilient individuals who will shape India's future through academic excellence, creative thinking, and ethical leadership.

Vision

To be recognized as a premier educational institution that develops global citizens rooted in Indian values, equipped with 21st century skills, and committed to sustainable development.

Our Core Values

Excellence

Pursuit of the highest standards in academics and character

Integrity

Commitment to truth, honesty, and ethical conduct

Global Mindset

Embracing diversity with respect for all cultures
