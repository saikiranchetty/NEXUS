Our Valuation Approach

Professional methods to determine your property's market value

Comparative Market Analysis

We analyze recent sales of similar properties in your neighborhood, adjusting for differences in features and condition to determine a competitive price range.

Property Assessment

Detailed evaluation of your home's size, layout, condition, upgrades, and unique features compared to others in the local market.

Neighborhood Trends

Review of local market conditions, inventory levels, buyer demand, and economic factors to position your property competitively.

What's Included in Your Valuation Report

Our comprehensive reports provide all the information you need to make informed decisions about your property.

  • Estimated current market value range - Precise pricing based on recent sales
  • Recent comparable sales data - Properties similar to yours that recently sold
  • Days on market analysis - How quickly homes are selling in your area
  • Price per square foot trends - Current valuation metrics for your neighborhood
  • Neighborhood market conditions - Supply, demand, and pricing trends
