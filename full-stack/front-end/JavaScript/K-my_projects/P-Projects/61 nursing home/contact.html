Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a tour of the facility?

You can schedule a tour by calling our admissions office at (555) 123-ADMIT, filling out the contact form on this page, or emailing tours@serenitygrove.com. We offer tours Monday through Friday from 9am to 5pm, and special weekend appointments can be arranged.

What are the visiting hours for residents?

Our general visiting hours are Monday-Friday from 9am to 8pm and weekends from 10am to 6pm. However, we understand that special circumstances may require flexible arrangements. Please speak with our staff to make special visiting arrangements if needed.

How do I get information about admission and pricing?

For detailed information about our admission process and pricing, please contact our admissions department at (555) 123-ADMIT or admissions@serenitygrove.com. We'll provide you with a detailed information packet and schedule a personalized consultation to discuss your specific needs and financial options.

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place?

We follow all CDC and local health department guidelines to ensure the safety of our residents and staff. Current measures include vaccination requirements for staff, regular testing, enhanced cleaning protocols, and visitor screening. Please contact us for our most current safety protocols.

What types of care do you provide?

We offer a range of care options including independent living, assisted living, memory care, and respite care. Our team works with each resident to create a personalized care plan that meets their specific needs and preferences.

Providing exceptional senior care with compassion and dignity since 1995. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for our residents through personalized care in a warm, homelike environment.
