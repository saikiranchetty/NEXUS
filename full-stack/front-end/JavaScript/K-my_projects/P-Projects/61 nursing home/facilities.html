Designed for Comfort & Safety

Our 5-acre campus features beautifully landscaped grounds and thoughtfully designed living spaces that combine comfort, safety, and accessibility. Every detail has been considered to create an environment that feels like home while meeting the unique needs of seniors.

Living Spaces

Private accommodations designed for comfort and independence

Private Rooms

Spacious private rooms with ensuite bathrooms, emergency call systems, and customizable layouts.

  • 250-300 sq ft
  • Private bathroom
  • Adjustable beds
  • Wi-Fi included

Semi-Private Rooms

Comfortable shared rooms with privacy curtains and ample space for personal belongings.

  • 300-350 sq ft
  • Shared bathroom
  • Privacy curtains
  • Individual storage

Memory Care Suites

Secure suites designed specifically for residents with dementia, featuring calming colors and wayfinding cues.

  • 200-250 sq ft
  • Secure environment
  • Visual cues
  • Monitoring system

Safety & Accessibility Features

Our facility incorporates the latest safety technologies and accessibility features to ensure resident wellbeing:

  • 24/7 emergency call system
  • Handrails in all hallways
  • Non-slip flooring
  • Wheelchair accessibility
  • Secure memory care unit
  • Fire safety system
  • Backup power generator
  • Medication management

24/7 Security

Monitored entrances and security cameras in common areas

Registered nurses available around the clock
