Our Agricultural Journey

Founded in 2008, GreenHarvest has grown from a small family farm to a leading agricultural enterprise serving communities across India. Our story is one of passion, innovation, and commitment to sustainable farming practices that nourish both people and the planet.

What began as a 5-acre organic farm has blossomed into a network of over 500 acres of cultivated land, supporting more than 1,000 small farmers through our cooperative programs. We take pride in bridging traditional farming wisdom with modern agricultural technologies.

Our Mission

To revolutionize agriculture through sustainable practices that increase farmer prosperity while protecting our environment. We are committed to producing nutritious, chemical-free food that nourishes communities and preserves biodiversity for future generations.

Through education, innovation, and fair trade, we empower small farmers to thrive in an evolving agricultural landscape.

Our Vision

To create a world where agriculture sustains both people and planet. We envision an India where every farmer can earn a dignified living through ecologically sound practices, where consumers have access to affordable, nutritious food, and where rural communities flourish.

By 2030, we aim to transform 10,000 acres to organic cultivation and train 5,000 farmers in sustainable techniques.

Our Humble Beginnings

GreenHarvest was founded by agricultural scientist Dr. Rajesh Patel with a simple goal: to prove that organic farming could be both environmentally sustainable and economically viable for small farmers.

2008

Established our first 5-acre organic demonstration farm in Punjab, focusing on wheat and vegetable cultivation.

2012

Launched farmer training programs and expanded to 50 acres with 15 partner farmers.

2015

Opened our first farmer's market outlet and began supplying to organic stores in major cities.

2020

Reached 500 acres under cultivation with 200+ partner farmers across 3 states.

2023

Launched our e-commerce platform and expanded exports to international markets.

Our Core Values

Principles that guide everything we do

Sustainability

We prioritize farming methods that regenerate the land rather than deplete it, ensuring long-term productivity and ecological balance.

Community

We believe in empowering farming communities through knowledge sharing, fair wages, and cooperative economic models.

Innovation

We combine traditional wisdom with cutting-edge agricultural technology to develop solutions tailored to local conditions.

Integrity
