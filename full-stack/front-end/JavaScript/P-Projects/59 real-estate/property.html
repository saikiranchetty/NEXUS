Description

This stunning luxury villa in the heart of Beverly Hills offers the perfect combination of modern elegance and comfort. Situated on a quiet, tree-lined street, this property boasts breathtaking views and unparalleled craftsmanship.

The open floor plan features a grand living room with floor-to-ceiling windows, a gourmet chef's kitchen with top-of-the-line appliances, and a spacious dining area perfect for entertaining. The master suite is a true retreat with a spa-like bathroom and a private balcony.

Outdoor living is at its finest with a resort-style pool, outdoor kitchen, and multiple seating areas. The professionally landscaped grounds provide privacy and tranquility while being just minutes from world-class shopping and dining on Rodeo Drive.

Additional features include a home theater, wine cellar, smart home technology, and a 2-car garage with electric vehicle charging station. This is a rare opportunity to own a piece of Beverly Hills paradise.
