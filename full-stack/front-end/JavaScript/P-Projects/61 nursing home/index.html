Welcome to Serenity Gardens

Where compassionate care meets comfortable living for your loved ones.

At Serenity Gardens, we understand that choosing a nursing home is one of the most important decisions you'll make for your family. Our mission is to provide exceptional care in a warm, homelike environment where residents can thrive physically, emotionally, and socially.

Our dedicated team of healthcare professionals is committed to meeting each resident's unique needs while preserving their dignity and independence. We offer a range of services from assisted living to specialized memory care, all designed to enhance quality of life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our care

We offer a full continuum of care including assisted living, memory care for residents with Alzheimer's and dementia, rehabilitation services (physical, occupational, and speech therapy), and long-term skilled nursing care. Our services are tailored to meet each resident's individual needs.

We have licensed nurses on staff 24/7 to handle any medical situations that arise. For emergencies, we have protocols in place to immediately contact emergency services while providing appropriate first response care. We also maintain close relationships with local hospitals and physicians.

Our staffing ratios exceed state requirements. During daytime hours, we maintain a ratio of at least 1 staff member for every 6 residents in assisted living and 1:4 in our memory care unit. At night, these ratios are 1:8 and 1:6 respectively. Additional staff are always available as needed.
