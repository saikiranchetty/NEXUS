Our Commitment to Staff

We invest in our team because we know that happy, well-trained staff provide the best care. Our staff benefits include:

  • Comprehensive training: 80+ hours of initial training and ongoing education
  • Competitive compensation: Salaries above industry average with regular reviews
  • Career advancement: Tuition reimbursement and promotion opportunities
  • Work-life balance: Flexible scheduling and generous PTO

Dr. Sarah Chen

Medical Director

Board Certified Geriatrician

Dr. Chen brings over 20 years of experience in geriatric medicine to Serenity Gardens. She completed her medical degree at Johns Hopkins University and her geriatrics fellowship at Mayo Clinic. Dr. Chen is passionate about holistic approaches to senior care that prioritize both physical health and quality of life.

Specialties:
  • Geriatric assessment
  • Polypharmacy management
  • Dementia care
  • Palliative medicine

Providing exceptional senior care with compassion and dignity since 1995. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for our residents through personalized care in a warm, homelike environment.
