Personalized Care Services

At Serenity Gardens, we offer a full continuum of care designed to meet the changing needs of our residents. From assistance with daily activities to specialized medical care, our services are delivered with compassion and respect for each individual's preferences and abilities.

Assisted Living

Our assisted living services provide the perfect balance of independence and support for seniors who need help with daily activities but don't require intensive medical care.

Services Include:

  • Personalized care plans
  • Assistance with bathing, dressing, and grooming
  • Medication management
  • Three nutritious meals daily plus snacks
  • Housekeeping and laundry services
  • 24-hour staff availability
  • Scheduled transportation
Learn More About Assisted Living

Memory Care

Our specialized memory care unit provides a secure, supportive environment for residents with Alzheimer's disease and other forms of dementia.

Program Features:

  • Specially trained staff with dementia expertise
  • Secure environment with monitored exits
  • Structured daily routine to reduce anxiety
  • Cognitive stimulation activities
  • Family support and education programs
  • Personalized sensory stimulation
Our memory care program follows the latest evidence-based practices in dementia care, focusing on maintaining residents' abilities and quality of life.
Learn More About Memory Care

Rehabilitation Services

Our comprehensive rehabilitation program helps residents recover strength, mobility, and independence after illness, injury, or surgery.

Therapy Services:

  • Physical Therapy
  • Occupational Therapy
  • Speech Therapy
  • Cardiac Rehab
  • Post-Stroke Rehab
  • Pain Management

Our Approach:

Our licensed therapists create personalized treatment plans with measurable goals. Therapy sessions are conducted in our state-of-the-art rehab gym or in residents' rooms as needed.

Learn More About Rehabilitation

Additional Services

Comprehensive care for all aspects of senior living

Nutrition & Dining

Our chef-prepared meals accommodate special dietary needs and preferences. We offer restaurant-style dining with menu choices at every meal.

  • Three meals plus snacks daily
  • Special diet accommodations
  • Nutrition counseling available

Activities & Social Programs

Our full-time activities director plans a diverse calendar of events to keep residents engaged physically, mentally, and socially.

  • Daily exercise classes
  • Arts and crafts
  • Outings and entertainment

Medical Services

We coordinate with physicians and specialists to provide comprehensive medical care on-site.

  • On-site physician visits
  • Medication management
  • 24-hour nursing care

Wellness Programs

Holistic approaches to maintain health and wellbeing through various therapeutic modalities.

  • Massage therapy
  • Aromatherapy
  • Music and pet therapy

Respite Care

Short-term stays available to give family caregivers a needed break while ensuring their loved ones receive quality care.

  • Flexible stay durations
  • Full access to amenities
  • Same services as long-term residents

Spiritual Care

We support residents' spiritual needs through various programs and services.

  • Weekly religious services
  • Pastoral visits
  • Meditation and prayer groups

Levels of Care

We adjust services as residents' needs change
