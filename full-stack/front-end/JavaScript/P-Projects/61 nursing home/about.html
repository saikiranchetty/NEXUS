Our Story

Founded in 1995 by Dr. Eleanor Richards, a geriatric specialist, Serenity Gardens began with a simple mission: to create a senior care community that felt like home while providing exceptional medical care.

Dr. Richards envisioned a place where seniors could maintain their dignity and independence while receiving the support they needed. What started as a 20-bed facility has grown into a 120-resident community with multiple specialized care units, but our core values remain unchanged.

Over the years, we've expanded our services and facilities, but our commitment to personalized, compassionate care has never wavered. We're proud to have served over 2,000 residents and their families throughout our history.

Dr. Eleanor Richards - Our Founder

Dr. Eleanor Richards (1945-2018) was a pioneer in geriatric care who dedicated her life to improving the quality of life for seniors. After completing her medical degree at Johns Hopkins University, she specialized in geriatrics at a time when few physicians focused on this growing population.

Her vision for Serenity Gardens came from personal experience caring for her aging parents and seeing the lack of quality options that balanced medical care with a homelike environment. She believed passionately that seniors deserved both excellent healthcare and the dignity of choice in their daily lives.

Even after retiring from active practice in 2010, Dr. Richards remained involved with Serenity Gardens as an advisor until her passing in 2018. Her legacy continues to guide our community today.

"Aging is not lost youth but a new stage of opportunity and strength."

Dr. Eleanor Richards
