About Sunrise Hospital

Our Overview

Founded in 1995, Sunrise Hospital is a premier multi-specialty healthcare provider serving thousands of patients annually. With a 500-bed capacity and cutting-edge medical technology, we are committed to delivering exceptional care across all medical disciplines.

Our Values

  • Compassionate Care
  • Medical Excellence
  • Patient-Centric Approach
  • Integrity and Transparency

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be the most trusted healthcare provider in the region, setting benchmarks in quality and innovation.

Mission: To deliver compassionate, high-quality care using advanced technology while ensuring affordability and accessibility.

CEO's Message

"At Sunrise Hospital, our goal is to transform lives through personalized care and advanced medical solutions. We are dedicated to ensuring every patient leaves healthier and happier." - Dr. Anil Kumar, CEO
