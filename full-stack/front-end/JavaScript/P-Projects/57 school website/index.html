About Our School

Established in 1995, Sunrise Public School has been a beacon of excellence in education, nurturing young minds to become responsible global citizens. Our student-centered approach focuses on holistic development through academics, sports, arts, and community service.

With a sprawling 10-acre campus, state-of-the-art facilities, and a team of dedicated educators, we provide an environment where every child can discover their potential and excel in their chosen path.

Why Choose Sunrise?

Expert Faculty

Our teachers are highly qualified with 85% holding postgraduate degrees and specialized training in child-centered pedagogy.

STEM Focus

Advanced laboratories and robotics programs prepare students for careers in science and technology.

Proven Results

Well-being

Comprehensive counseling services and wellness programs support students' mental health.

Academic Programs

Primary School (I-V)

Our play-based learning approach builds strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and social skills through experiential activities.

  • Activity-based curriculum
  • Language immersion program
  • Personalized learning plans
Middle School (VI-VIII)

Transitioning to subject specialists while maintaining interdisciplinary connections and project-based learning.

  • STEM enrichment programs
  • Leadership development
  • Career exploration
High School (IX-XII)

Rigorous academic preparation with multiple streams (Science, Commerce, Humanities) and university counseling.

  • Advanced placement courses
  • Research opportunities
  • College prep workshops
