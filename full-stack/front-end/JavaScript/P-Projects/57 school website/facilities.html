Academic Facilities

Smart Classrooms

Technology-enabled learning spaces with interactive whiteboards, projectors, and audio-visual equipment to enhance the teaching-learning experience.

  • 65" Interactive LED panels
  • Digital content repository
  • Ergonomic furniture
Science Laboratories

Well-equipped physics, chemistry, and biology labs that allow students to experiment and explore scientific concepts hands-on.

  • Separate labs for each discipline
  • Modern equipment and apparatus
  • Safety-certified infrastructure
Resource Center & Library

A knowledge hub with over 25,000 books, periodicals, digital resources, and quiet study spaces to foster a love for reading.

  • Print and digital resources
  • Reading lounge areas
  • Research workstations

Technology Facilities

Computer Labs

Four air-conditioned computer labs with 200+ workstations equipped with the latest hardware and educational software.

Robotics Lab

Dedicated space for robotics and AI education with Arduino kits, 3D printers, and programming workstations.

VR Learning Center

Immersive virtual reality setups for experiential learning in science, history, and geography.

Sports & Recreation

Swimming Pool

Olympic-standard 25-meter pool with trained coaches and separate timings for different age groups.

Indoor Sports Complex

Badminton, basketball, table tennis, and gymnastics facilities with professional-grade flooring.

Athletics Track

400-meter synthetic track with field events area for javelin, shot put, long jump, and high jump.

Chess & Mind Sports

Dedicated area for chess, carrom, and other mind sports with regular tournaments.

Performing Arts Studio

Spaces for music, dance, and drama with practice rooms and performance areas.

  • Annual art exhibition
  • Guest artist workshops
  • Digital art workstations

Auditorium

600-seat auditorium with professional lighting and sound systems for performances, assemblies, and guest lectures.

  • Annual drama productions
  • Inter-school competitions
  • Film screenings
